The Universty Of Jordan A Top 500 University
The University of Jordan(UJ), established in 1962 as Jordan's first university, has blossomed into a national leader, claiming the #1 spot in the country and even the 10th position in the Middle East. Since 2018, it has proudly held a 5-star QS rating, culminating in its prestigious entry into the QS World University Rankings top 500 in 2023. However, UJ's allure extends far beyond rankings. Its vibrant campus hums with a diverse community of 60,000 students from over 130 nations, united by 240 distinct programs across 19 schools. Moreover,UJ distinguishes itself as a top employer, ranking within the prestigious top 250 globally for employee reputation, a testament to its commitment to fostering a rewarding work environment.
